Attractions

Argao is known for its historic and natural attractions. The municipality offers a variety of options that will satisfy every tourist's needs and wants for relaxation and enjoyment. Below are some of the most famous wonders of Argao:

Balay sa Agta Cave

Balay sa Agta Cave is a very interesting cave formed and can be found in Argao.

Bugasok Falls

Bugasok Falls is a refreshing natural falls located in the mountains of Argao.

Cabecera de Argao

Cabecera de Argao, the fortified town center and seat of the Argao municipal government.

Mahayahay Beach Resort

Mahayahay Beach Resort is a very popular beach in Argao, Cebu Province.

Liki Cold Spring

The Liki Cold Spring is located in Barangay Taloot of the town.

Coal Mountain Resort

This is a relatively new nature site in Argao.

Taloot Mangrove Forest

Taloot Mangrove Forest has centuries old mangrove trees.

Sumbria River

At Sombria River, there are 20 species of birds and exotic animals.
